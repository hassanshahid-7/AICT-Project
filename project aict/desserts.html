Classic Tiramisu

Ingredients: Ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, eggs, sugar, coffee, cocoa powder

Instructions: Brew strong coffee and allow it to cool. Whisk egg yolks and sugar, then add mascarpone cheese. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites and fold into the mascarpone mixture. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer them in a dish. Spread mascarpone mixture on top. Repeat layers and refrigerate for 4 hours. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
