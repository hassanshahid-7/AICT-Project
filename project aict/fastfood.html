Loaded Fries

Ingredients:1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or cheese of your choice) 1/2 cup cooked bacon bits (or crumbled bacon) 1/4 cup green onions, chopped 1/2 cup sour cream 1/4 cup jalapeño slices (optional) 1/4 cup diced tomatoes (optional) 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (optional)

Instructions:Spread the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, making sure they aren't crowded to get crispy edges. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crispy.While the fries are baking, cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Once done, crumble or chop it into small pieces. Chop the green onions, slice the jalapeños (if using), and dice the tomatoes.Drizzle sour cream over the loaded fries and garnish with chopped cilantro for an added touch of flavor..

Creamy Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients: 1 cup heavy cream 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese 1/2 teaspoon salt (to taste) 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (to taste) 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional, but adds depth of flavor) Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish, optional)

Instructions: In a large skillet or saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant, but not browned. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer. Stir frequently to prevent the cream from burning. Once simmering, reduce the heat to low and gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese. Stir until the cheese melts completely and the sauce becomes smooth and creamy. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg (optional) to taste.Add the cooked pasta directly into the Alfredo sauce, tossing gently to coat the noodles with the creamy sauce. If the sauce seems too thick, add a little bit of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up. Stir until the pasta is well coated with the sauce.

Dumplings

Ingredients: 2 cups all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup water1/2 lb (225g) ground pork (or chicken, beef, or tofu for a vegetarian option) 1/4 cup finely chopped cabbage (bok choy, napa cabbage, or regular cabbage) 2 tablespoons green onions, chopped 1 tablespoon soy sauce 1 tablespoon sesame oil 1 teaspoon grated ginger 1 teaspoon garlic, minced 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Gradually add the water and vegetable oil, stirring continuously until a dough forms.n a mixing bowl, combine the ground pork (or your chosen protein), chopped cabbage, green onions, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, salt, pepper, and sugar. Mix everything together until well combined. Make sure the mixture is evenly seasoned and moist. You can taste the filling by cooking a small spoonful in a pan to check for seasoning.

Chicken Cheese Pizza

Ingredients: 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet) 1 1/2 cups warm water (about 110°F/45°C) 1 teaspoon sugar 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (fresh or shredded) Toppings of choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, sausage, etc.) Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish) Olive oil (for drizzling, optional)

Instructions:In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir the ingredients together until the dough begins to form. Then, knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.Using a spoon or ladle, spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce over the rolled-out dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted. The edges should be crispy and slightly charred.

Beef Cheese Burger

Ingredients: 4 burger buns 4 slices of cheddar cheese (or cheese of your choice) 1 large tomato, sliced 1 red onion, thinly sliced Lettuce leaves (such as iceberg or romaine) Pickles (optional) Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise (optional)

Instructions: Place the bottom half of the toasted bun on a plate. Add a layer of lettuce, followed by the cooked beef patty with melted cheese. Top with sliced tomato, onions, and pickles. Spread your choice of ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise on the top bun and place it on top of the assembled ingredients.
