Popular Recipes

Hyderabadi Biryani

Ingredients: Rice, Meat (Chicken/Mutton/Goat), Yogurt, Spices (Ginger, Garlic, Green Chillies, Turmeric, Cumin, Coriander, Saffron), Onions, Tomatoes, etc.

Recipe:Marinate the Meat: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, salt, cilantro, and mint. Add chicken, mix well, and refrigerate for 1-4 hours. Cook the Rice: Boil water with whole spices and salt. Add soaked basmati rice, cook 70% of the way, then drain. Fry the Onions: Fry sliced onions in ghee/oil until golden brown and set aside. Cook the Meat: In a pan, cook marinated chicken until tender, about 10-15 minutes. Layer the Biryani: In a pot, layer half the rice, then the cooked meat, then the remaining rice. Sprinkle fried onions, saffron milk, and fresh herbs. Steam (Dum): Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 25-30 minutes.

White Karahi

Ingredients: Chicken/Mutton, Yogurt, Ginger-Garlic Paste, Cream, Onions, Tomatoes, Spices (Cumin, Coriander, Garam Masala)

Recipe: Cook Chicken: Heat ghee/oil in a karahi or large pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes. Add Spices & Chicken: Add black pepper, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Stir in the chicken pieces and cook until browned. Add Yogurt & Cream: Lower the heat and add whisked yogurt, mixing well. Cook for 5 minutes until it releases oil. Add cream and stir. If the curry is too thick, add a little water to adjust consistency. Simmer: Let the karahi cook for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is tender and the gravy thickens. Garnish & Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.

Ras Malai

Ingredients: Milk, Paneer, Sugar, Saffron, Cardamom, Pistachios, Almonds

Recipe:Prepare Milk: Boil milk in a pan. Once it thickens slightly, add sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron milk. Stir and cook for 5-7 minutes. Soak Rasgullas: Gently squeeze the rasgullas to remove excess syrup. Add them to the milk mixture and cook for 3-4 minutes. Chill & Serve: Let the ras malai cool. Chill in the refrigerator for an hour. Garnish: Garnish with chopped pistachios and almonds.

White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients: Pasta, Butter, Milk, Flour, Garlic, Onion, Salt, Pepper, Parmesan Cheese

Recipe:Cook Pasta: Boil pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. Make White Sauce: In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until it forms a paste. Gradually add milk while whisking to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens. Add Cheese & Seasoning: Stir in grated Parmesan, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Combine: Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until well coated. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Mint Margarita

Ingredients: Tequila, Cointreau, Lime Juice, Mint Leaves, Sugar

Recipe:Muddle Mint: In a glass or shaker, muddle the fresh mint leaves with lime juice and sugar/simple syrup. Mix: Add tequila, triple sec, and ice cubes. Shake well or stir. Serve: Rim a glass with salt or sugar (optional), pour the mixture into the glass, and garnish with lime slices and mint sprigs.
